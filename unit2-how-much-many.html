How much water is there? ✓  ·  How much waters are… ✗

🔄 HOW MUCH vs HOW MANY
How much
is there? (rice = U)
Quanto riso c'è?
How many
are there? (carrots = C)
Quante carote ci sono?
How much
do you need? —
Quanto zucchero ti serve? — Poco.
How many
do you need? —
Quante uova ti servono? — Molte.
💡 Trick: much → U (non si conta) · many → C (si conta)
Dubbio? Chiedi: posso contarlo? → many. No? → much.
